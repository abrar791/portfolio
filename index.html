some of my projects

project 01
Snake Game

Revived the iconic Snake game in the browser, where the player controls a lengthening serpent munching pellets amid escalating speed, dodging walls and self-collisions for high scores.

project 02
Calculator

A sleek, responsive calculator app that handles basic arithmetic, advanced functions like sqrt and percentages, and a clean interface for quick computations on any device.

project 03
Rock Paper Scissors

Classic Rock Paper Scissors showdown in the browser: Players choose rock, paper, or scissors against a smart AI opponent, tallying wins, losses, and ties with instant feedback and score resets.

project 04
Flappy bird

Recreated the infamously addictive Flappy Bird as a browser-based challenge, where players tap to navigate a bird through endless pipes, battling gravity for the ultimate high score.
